In this dataset, each entry represents a person who takes a credit by a bank. 
Each person is classified as good or bad credit risks according to the set of attributes. 

Numerical attributes:
Age
Job: (numeric: 0 - unskilled and non-resident, 1 - unskilled and resident, 2 - skilled, 3 - highly skilled)
Credit_amount: Credit taken by individual
Duration

Categorical attributes:
Sex
Housing
Saving_account
Checking_account
Credit_cat
Purpose

Class label: Risk
1 = good credit (low risk)
0 = bad credit (high risk)
